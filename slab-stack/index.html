SlabStack: Infinite Isometric Stacker.

Master the ultimate test of timing and precision. Align moving slabs perfectly in gorgeous isometric 3D space across three distinct geometric modes. One wrong step, and your tower shrinks!

Play on Android View Modes

Pick Your Shape, Test Your Focus

SlabStack brings dynamic procedural color transitions and classic reflex mechanics together. Slices that over-extend are cut off cleanly—forcing your surface area to narrow as the game progresses.

◼

Classic Rectangle

The fundamental stacking test. Slabs swing dynamically from alternating orthogonal axes. Perfect for executing long streak combos and maximizing block surface area.

●

Conical Cylinder

Stack round structural pillars. The curvature adds a subtle layer of visual depth, narrowing down into clean geometric spires as your timing fragments the base disks.

▲

Prism Triangle

The ultimate trial. Triangular slices present extreme directional precision requirements. Misplacements exponentially slice down real estate, leading to rapid, high-stakes finishes.
