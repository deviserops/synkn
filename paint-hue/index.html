SAFETY ZONE

Parents' Corner & FAQs

Your privacy, offline availability, and drawing device compatibility questions resolved.

👶 Is PaintHue safe for children?

Absolutely! PaintHue is engineered under tight COPPA safety compliance guidelines. There are no unsolicited external redirect paths, and we prioritize a quiet sandbox interface that minimizes overstimulation.

✈️ Can my child use PaintHue offline?

Yes! Once installed, all standard outlines, canvas frameworks, color coordination modules, and saved local levels function 100% offline. Perfect for flights, road trips, and locations without cellular connectivity.

🎨 What drawing devices are officially supported?

PaintHue supports direct multi-touch inputs, generic capacitive stylus pens, Apple Pencil (iPadOS), and Samsung S-Pen devices. Fine pressure-sensitivity automatically adapts depending on your input hardware!

🎒 Are there custom coloring books for educational themes?

Yes, our level packs are sorted into educational directories (Animals, Nature, Spaces, Geometry). This helps children recognize vocabulary terms and nature structures as they play.
