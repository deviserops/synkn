Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

At GlowSync, accessible via our mobile application and our web services hosted by Synkn ("Developer", "We", "Us", or "Our"), user privacy is a paramount priority. This Global Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, process, secure, and disclose your data in compliance with international privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), and applicable regional digital acts.

1. Information We Collect

To facilitate a localized marketplace between Customers and Providers, GlowSync collects the following categories of information:

  • Account Parameters: Real names, verified active phone numbers, and profile emails.
  • Precise Geolocation Data (Sensitive Personal Information): With your explicit operating system consent, we access real-time GPS coordinates via Flutter frameworks to compute physical distances between users and local salon storefronts. This data is utilized strictly for real-time proximity matching and is not used to build historical location profiles.
  • System Identifiers: Secure tokens stored via device storage maps (such as flutter_secure_storage) to securely maintain active authenticated user sessions.

2. Legal Basis for Processing & Data Retention

If you reside within the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, our legal grounds for processing your data depend on the context:

  • Performance of a Contract: Processing account data is mandatory to deliver the marketplace coordination and booking calendar systems you request.
  • Consent: Accessing your device's precise live location relies entirely on your explicit permission granted via system popup parameters. You can revoke this anytime in your device settings.
  • Legitimate Interests: To detect app fraud, prevent malicious spam bookings, and monitor server health.

Data Retention Window: We retain your personal data only for as long as your user account remains active or as required to fulfill the business purposes outlined in this policy. Deleted account data is purged completely within 72 hours, except where retention is legally mandated.

3. Data Integrity & Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. GlowSync does not disclose your information to third parties for their independent marketing or commercial purposes. We utilize only necessary internal mapping and UI framework components (such as flutter_map, latlong2, and geolocator) to render local service availability and distances within the application.

4. Your Global Privacy Rights

Regardless of your residency, Synkn honors comprehensive consumer rights regarding digital data:

  • The Right to Erasure (Deletion): You have the absolute right to clear your entire cloud identity profile from our database.
  • The Right to Access & Portability: You may request a plain copy of the profile telemetry variables associated with your mobile identity.

5. Google Play Compliant Data Deletion Protocol

In strict adherence to Google Play Console Developer Account Deletion mandates, GlowSync provides an integrated, automated web portal mechanism for purging structural user properties.

🔗 Access Secure Account Purge Portal (https://api.synkn.app/account-delete)

Executing this sequence triggers an automated cascade deleting your email, name, phone tracking metrics, and historical marketplace records directly from active production clusters within 72 hours.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA/GDPR Compliance)

GlowSync does not knowingly solicit, target, or collect structural telemetry identifiers from individuals under the age of 13 (or 16 within the EEA). If we discover that an underage individual has generated an account, we will purge those specific database rows immediately.

7. Changes to This Global Policy

We may refresh our privacy architecture from time to time to match global regulatory shifts or updated Flutter SDK versions. We will indicate structural changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the absolute header of this document.

8. Privacy Contact & Data Disputes

To execute your global consumer rights, appeal a data processing sequence, or seek manual data lookups, please contact our dedicated Privacy Compliance Team.
